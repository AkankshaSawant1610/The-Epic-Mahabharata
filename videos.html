Bhishma Parva: Chapter 25

The Bhagavad Gita - Krishna's Divine Counsel

Part 6 of 18 33 min 28 sec Kurukshetra

In this pivotal chapter of the Bhishma Parva, Arjuna is overcome with moral dilemma at the prospect of battling his own kin. Lord Krishna imparts the timeless wisdom of the Bhagavad Gita, revealing profound truths about duty (dharma), the eternal soul, and the path to liberation (moksha).

"कर्मण्येवाधिकारस्ते मा फलेषु कदाचन।
मा कर्मफलहेतुर्भूर्मा ते सङ्गोऽस्त्वकर्मणि॥"

"You have a right to perform your prescribed duty, but you are not entitled to the fruits of action. Never consider yourself the cause of the results of your activities, and never be attached to not doing your duty."
